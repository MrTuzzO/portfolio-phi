Ride sharing app |

The ride-sharing app designed to connect passengers with nearby drivers for on-demand transportation. The app offers a simple and intuitive interface where users can easily book rides, view available drivers in real-time, and track the driver's location during the journey. It supports various ride options, from economical carpooling to premium vehicles, allowing users to choose based on their preferences or budget.

Key features include integrated GPS navigation for route tracking, fare estimation based on distance and time, and secure in-app payments. The app also enables users to rate drivers and view feedback, ensuring a reliable and quality service. Built with a focus on convenience and efficiency, the platform aims to streamline the urban transportation experience by making ride booking fast, safe, and hassle-free.

Blood Bank Management System |

The Blood Bank Management System to efficiently manage blood donations, donor information, and blood stock availability. The system streamlines the process of registering donors, tracking donations, and maintaining a real-time inventory of blood units. It features an intuitive interface for both staff and administrators to manage donor records, blood types, and donation history.

Key functionalities include donor registration, blood requests from hospitals, and alerts for low blood stock levels. The system automatically updates available blood types and quantities after each donation or issue, ensuring accurate stock management. It also incorporates a search feature to quickly locate specific blood types and a reporting module to generate summaries of donation activities and stock levels.
